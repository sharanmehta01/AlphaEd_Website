Our Journey

Team Formation

October 2024

A group of passionate educators and technologists came together with a shared vision to revolutionize personalized learning through AI. The concept of AlphaEd was born.

Company Founded

January 2025

AlphaEd was officially incorporated, bringing together expertise in education, artificial intelligence, and learning science to create the next generation of educational technology.

Product Launch

April 2025

Successfully launched our flagship AI-powered learning platform, introducing adaptive learning pathways and real-time analytics for educators.

Series A Funding

May 2025

Secured significant investment to accelerate product development and expand our reach to more educational institutions globally.
