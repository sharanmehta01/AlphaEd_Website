Frequently Asked Questions

How long does implementation take?

Typical implementation takes 2-4 weeks, depending on your institution's size and requirements. This includes:

  • Initial setup and configuration: 3-5 days
  • Data migration and integration: 1-2 weeks
  • User training and onboarding: 1 week
  • Testing and final adjustments: 3-5 days

Is training included?

Yes, comprehensive training is included for all user levels:

  • Administrator training sessions
  • Teacher workshops and resources
  • Student orientation materials
  • Access to our online learning portal
  • Ongoing support and refresher courses

Can it integrate with existing systems?

Yes, our platform integrates seamlessly with most major systems including:

  • Learning Management Systems (Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle)
  • Student Information Systems
  • Authentication systems (SSO, LDAP)
  • Third-party educational tools and APIs

What kind of support do you offer?

We provide comprehensive support through multiple channels:

  • 24/7 technical support via chat and email
  • Dedicated customer success manager
  • Regular check-ins and progress reviews
  • Online knowledge base and documentation
  • Priority phone support during business hours

How do you ensure data privacy?

We take data privacy seriously and maintain strict security measures:

  • GDPR and FERPA compliance
  • End-to-end encryption
  • Regular security audits
  • Secure data centers
  • Data backup and recovery protocols

What are the system requirements?

Our platform is web-based and works on most modern devices:

  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Stable internet connection (minimum 2 Mbps)
  • Any device with 4GB RAM or more
  • Supports both desktop and mobile devices
